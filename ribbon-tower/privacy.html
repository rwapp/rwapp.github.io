Ribbon Tower — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

The Ribbon Tower app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third‑party tracking. The only time we receive anything from you is if you choose to email us feedback — see “Feedback you send us” below.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no sign‑in, collects no personal information, and contains no analytics or tracking software of any kind. We have no servers that record anything about you and no way to identify you.

Network requests

To show you a daily memory, the app downloads content over the internet:

  • A small content file and the images it references are fetched from our static host (rwapp.co.uk) and from the public image sources those memories link to. As with any internet request, the host receiving it can see your device's IP address — this is a standard part of how the internet works and is not used by us to identify or track you.
  • Opening a recommended article, photo credit, or video launches that content from its original provider (for example a news site, a photo‑sharing site, or YouTube) inside the app. Those providers have their own privacy practices, which we don't control. Please refer to their policies.

Data stored on your device

The app keeps a copy of the most recent content on your device so it works offline and loads quickly. This stays on your device, is never sent anywhere, and is removed when you delete the app.

Sharing

When you choose to share a memory, the app creates an image on your device and hands it to the system share sheet. What happens next is entirely your choice and is handled by whichever app or service you share to — Ribbon Tower is not involved beyond creating the image.

Feedback you send us

The app's “Send feedback” option, and emailing us directly, both open an email to ribbontower@rwapp.co.uk. This is entirely optional. If you send one, we receive your email address and whatever you write in the message — because that's simply how email works.

We use that only to read and reply to your feedback. We don't add you to any mailing list, share your address, or use it for anything else. We keep feedback emails only as long as useful to deal with them, then delete them. You can ask us to delete an email you've sent by writing to the same address.

Children

The app collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email ribbontower@rwapp.co.uk.
