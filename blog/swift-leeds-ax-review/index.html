Daniel Devesa Derksen-Staats

Dani is having a blast working at Yoto!

Previously, he’s loved working at Apple, Spotify, Skyscanner, and the BBC, where he gained valuable experience making iOS apps more inclusive and fostering organisational cultures that prioritise accessibility.

He is the author of the book “Developing Accessible iOS Apps”, and likes to keep himself busy by writing iOS and accessibility tips on social media with the hashtag #365DaysIOSAccessibility.

Rob Whitaker

Rob Whitaker is a senior iOS engineer at Rightmove and an iOS and macOS accessibility specialist. He’s worked on iOS at Capital One, Apple, and Cuvva.

What Rob loves most is talking about making Apple platforms more accessible. So much so that he wrote an entire book on the subject - “Developing Inclusive Mobile Apps”. He’s also provided accessibility rule definitions for iOS developer tool Reveal, and has provided consulting and training for major brands such as Spotify.
